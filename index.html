ABOUT AFRIKAN SON

Mr. Marks, the Afrikan Son

My name is Mark Wanjau and I am a musician and visual artist. In the field of music, I am a singer and multi-instrumentalis forged from the pits of despair and victory from challenges of becoming a music prodigy since the tender age 10. Acheiving numerous international certificates that I am now proud and fond of.

I also have an interest of creating amazing, radiant (albeit others are shadowy), subtle and contrasting types of art. They range from painting, digital art and graffitti art.

I do also have a passion for writing. Delving into and making use of language to its intrinsic words has a particular relish that excites my word-play palate.
