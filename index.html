ABOUT AFRIKAN SON

Mr. Marks, the Afrikan Son

My name is Mark Wanjau and I am a musician and visual artist. In the field of music, I am a singer and multi-instrumentalist forged from the pits of despair and victory from challenges of becoming a music prodigy since the tender age of 10. I have acheived numerous international certificates that I am now proud and fond of. These certificates have been officiated by The Associated Board of Royal Schools of Music(ABRSM) of which its headquarters are located in the United Kingdom.

I also have an interest of creating amazing, radiant (albeit others are shadowy), subtle and contrasting types of art. They range from painting, digital art and graffitti art.

I do also have a passion for writing. Delving into and making use of language to its intrinsic words has a particular relish that excites my word-play palate.

My hobbies and interests include in general:

  • Music playing and listening.
  • Visual Art
  • Baking and Cooking
  • Watching Documentaries ranging of all genres
  • Photography
